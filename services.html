We Are Family

  • In 2022, Sarah adopted Max, a shy golden retriever who had been abandoned. Max was timid and hesitant at first, but with Sarah's love and patience, he has blossomed into a playful and affectionate companion, always by her side.
  • Two years ago, Emily adopted Peanut, a rabbit who had been surrendered due to its previous owner's allergies. Peanut was quiet and anxious, but today he happily hops around Emily’s garden, enjoying a peaceful, loving home.
  • Last summer, James adopted Luna, a stray kitten found on the streets. Luna was sick and frail, but after months of care, she’s now a healthy, curious cat who loves lounging on James's lap, bringing joy to his everyday life.
